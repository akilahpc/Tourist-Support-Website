USA

The United States of America is the third most visited country in the world. About 75 million people spend their holidays in the USA each year. Many come to visit American national parks, some prefer American cities like Las Vegas, New York City or Los Angeles, while families are drawn to Disney parks. The USA has something exciting for everyone.

Without a doubt, the natural beauty climbs to the top of the reasons to visit the USA. Your chances of seeing wildlife are greater in America than in many other developed countries.

The United States enjoys all seasons and therefore is a great tourist destination year-round. The variety of recreational activities range from cave repelling to mountain climbing and anything in between. Hiking, kayaking, yachting, swimming, golfing, biking, skiing, and snowmobiling are always available somewhere in the United States.
